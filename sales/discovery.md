# Skill: Discovery

## What It Is
Discovery is the process of deeply understanding a prospect's business 
challenges, goals, and decision-making process before proposing a solution.

## When to Use It
- First and second sales calls with a new prospect
- When re-engaging a stalled opportunity
- When a deal has gone quiet and you need to requalify

## Key Behaviors
1. Ask open-ended questions before talking about your product
2. Understand the "why behind the why" — surface root causes, not symptoms
3. Map stakeholders and understand who has budget, authority, and urgency
4. Summarize back what you heard to confirm understanding

## Example Questions
- "What's driving the urgency to solve this now?"
- "What happens if this problem isn't addressed in the next 6 months?"
- "Who else is impacted by this challenge?"

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Pitching before you've finished listening
- Accepting surface-level answers without probing deeper
- Forgetting to ask about the decision process

## Success Looks Like
The rep can clearly articulate the prospect's top 3 pain points, their 
timeline, their budget range, and who signs the deal — before the demo.
